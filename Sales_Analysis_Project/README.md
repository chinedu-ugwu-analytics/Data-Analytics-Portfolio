##Project Overview

This project analyzes sales data to evaluate business performance, identify sales trends, and understand customer and product behavior. The goal is to support data-driven decision-making using clear insights and visualizations.

Problem Statement

The business wants to understand:

How sales are performing over time

Which products and regions generate the most revenue

Whether sales targets are being met

This analysis helps management improve sales strategy and forecasting.

#Tools Used

##Power BI
##Excel

#Dataset Description

The dataset contains sales transaction data including:

Date (day, month, week)

Product

Customer name

Country and region

Quantity sold

Unit price

Revenue

Monthly sales targets

Steps Performed

Cleaned and prepared the sales data

Built a relational data model using fact and dimension tables

Created key metrics such as total revenue, quantity sold, and sales vs target

Analyzed sales trends by time, product, customer, and region

Designed an interactive Power BI dashboard for reporting

Key Insights

A small number of products contribute the highest share of revenue

Sales peak during specific months and weeks

Some regions consistently outperform others

Sales targets are missed during low-demand periods

Conclusion

The analysis provides clear visibility into sales performance and highlights opportunities for improving product focus, regional strategy, and target setting. The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor performance and make informed business decisions.
