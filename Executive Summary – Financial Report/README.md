## Overview
This project delivers an executive-level financial summary designed to provide a clear and concise view of business performance. Using structured financial data and interactive Power BI visuals, the report highlights key revenue, cost, and profitability metrics to support high-level decision-making.

---

## Business Objective
The purpose of this report is to:
- Present a high-level financial overview for executives and stakeholders  
- Summarize sales, costs, and profitability across markets and products  
- Enable quick identification of performance trends and variances  
- Support strategic planning and financial decision-making  

---

## Tools Used
- Power BI  
- Excel  
- DAX (for financial calculations and measures)  

---

## Dataset Description
The dataset contains financial and sales performance data, including:
- Market Segment (Government, Midmarket, Enterprise, Small Business)  
- Country  
- Product  
- Units Sold  
- Manufacturing Price  
- Sales Price  
- Gross Price  
- Sales Revenue  
- Cost of Goods Sold (COGS)  
- Profit  
- Month-Year (time-based analysis)  

This dataset supports high-level financial reporting and profitability analysis.

---

## Methodology
The analysis followed a structured approach:
1. Cleaned and validated financial data for accuracy and consistency  
2. Calculated key financial KPIs such as revenue, COGS, profit, and margins  
3. Analyzed performance across market segments, products, and countries  
4. Evaluated profitability trends over time  
5. Designed an executive-focused Power BI dashboard for quick insights  

---

## Key Insights
- Profit contribution varies significantly across market segments  
- Certain products drive higher revenue but lower margins  
- Cost behavior plays a major role in overall profitability  
- Financial performance shows clear trends across reporting periods  

---

## Conclusion
This executive summary provides a concise and insightful financial overview that enables leadership to quickly assess performance, identify risks and opportunities, and make informed strategic decisions. The Power BI dashboard serves as a decision-support tool for executive-level reporting.

- Executive-level Power BI financial dashboard  
- Key financial KPIs and performance indicators  
- Visual summary for stakeholder reporting
