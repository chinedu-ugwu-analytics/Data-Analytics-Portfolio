## Overview
This project analyzes shipping and order data to evaluate delivery performance, customer segments, and sales patterns across regions. The analysis focuses on understanding how shipping modes, delivery timelines, and customer segments impact overall sales and operational efficiency.

---

## Business Objective
The objective of this analysis is to:
- Evaluate shipping performance and delivery timelines  
- Analyze sales distribution across customer segments and regions  
- Understand the impact of shipping modes on order fulfillment  
- Support data-driven decisions in logistics and sales strategy  

---

## Tools Used
- Excel
- Advanced Pivot Tables
- Statistics  

---

## Dataset Description
The dataset contains order and shipping-level information, including:
- Order Date  
- Shipping Date  
- Shipping Days (delivery duration)  
- Shipping Mode  
- Customer Name  
- Customer Segment (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office)  
- Country, Region, State, City  
- Product Category and Sub-Category  
- Sales and Average Sales  
- Month-Year (time-based analysis)  

This structure supports time-series, regional, and customer-segment analysis.

---

## Methodology
The analysis followed these steps:
1. Cleaned and standardized date and sales fields  
2. Calculated delivery duration using order and shipping dates  
3. Analyzed shipping performance by shipping mode  
4. Evaluated sales trends across customer segments and regions  
5. Compared average sales across product categories and sub-categories  
6. Built interactive visuals to highlight key logistics and sales insights  

---

## Key Insights
- Shipping duration varies significantly by shipping mode  
- Corporate and Consumer segments generate the highest sales volume  
- Certain regions consistently record higher shipping activity and revenue  
- Faster shipping modes are associated with higher average sales  
- Specific product categories drive the majority of shipping demand  

---

## Conclusion
The analysis provides insights into how shipping performance and customer segmentation influence sales outcomes. These findings can help businesses optimize shipping strategies, improve delivery efficiency, and tailor services to high-value customer segments.
